About Us

Café Friends is a cozy coffee shop in the heart of Riga, opened in 2026 with one simple idea: to be a welcoming place where good coffee and great company come together. We serve high-quality coffee made from carefully selected beans and homemade pastries baked fresh every day.

Our space is designed for comfort – soft lighting, green plants, and comfortable seating create the perfect spot to relax, work, read, or catch up with friends. Whether you're starting your day with a strong espresso, enjoying a creamy latte in the afternoon, or treating yourself to one of our cakes, we want every visit to feel warm and familiar.

We believe coffee is best when shared. That's why at Café Friends, you're not just a customer – you're part of our little community. Come as you are, stay as long as you like, and let us make your day a little better, one cup at a time.

We look forward to seeing you soon!
